Two worlds, one operator

By day: Project Administrator & Acquisitions at H.L. Dynasty, a Queens-based construction, brokerage, and development firm. The path: Born in Jakarta, raised in Hong Kong, home in New York City. Computer Science graduate of CUNY Queens College. My faith in Jesus is central to who I am, and I stay active in my church community. Outside of work: photography, and helping people think things through.
